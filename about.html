About Us

Leaf & Glow began with a love for all things natural. We wanted to create skincare that feels like self-care — simple, organic, and real. Our team works closely with herbalists and clean-ingredient chemists to deliver formulas that nourish and restore without compromise.

Our Mission

We’re here to prove that beauty doesn’t need to come at the cost of health or the environment. Every bottle we create is packed with nature’s best, responsibly sourced and thoughtfully packaged.
